Help & Rules

Terms of Interaction

This page defines communication standards, usage rights, privacy boundaries, and enforcement for this website and related interactions.

Help & Contact Policy

If you need to reach out regarding my work, projects, or this website, use official channels only. Contact methods are currently under development.

Before contacting, review the site and confirm your question is relevant. Messages that are vague, repetitive, or low-effort may be ignored.

Required Message Structure

  • Who you are.
  • Why you are contacting me.
  • The exact context and desired outcome.

Response Conditions

  • Responses are not guaranteed.
  • Replies happen at my discretion and timeline.
  • Multiple follow-ups do not increase priority.

Content Ownership & Usage

All content on this site is owned by me unless explicitly credited otherwise. This includes text, audio, music, visuals, concepts, and original design work.

What Is Not Allowed

  • Copying, downloading, reuploading, or redistributing content.
  • Removing attribution or claiming authorship.
  • Modifying, remixing, or adapting material without permission.
  • Commercial or promotional use without explicit approval.
  • Use in misleading, harmful, or defamatory contexts.

Limited Allowances

  • Sharing direct links to this website.
  • Referencing my work with clear credit.
  • Requesting written permission for specific usage.

Permission is never implied. Silence does not equal consent.

Collaboration & Requests

Collaboration is selective and must align with quality, relevance, and mutual value. Not all requests will receive a response.

Eligibility

  • Clear scope and realistic expectations.
  • Direct relevance to my work and direction.
  • Defined contributions and outcomes.

Not Accepted

  • Exposure-only offers.
  • Unpaid work with no value exchange.
  • Vague ideas without goals.
  • Pressure-based or urgency-manipulative requests.

Decision authority is final. I reserve the right to decline, end discussions, or withdraw from collaboration when boundaries are crossed.

Behavior & Conduct

All interaction must remain respectful, intentional, and in good faith. Disagreement does not permit disrespect.

Expected Conduct

  • Professional communication.
  • Respect for boundaries and decisions.
  • Good-faith engagement only.

Zero-Tolerance Behavior

  • Harassment, threats, abuse, or intimidation.
  • Hate speech, discrimination, or targeted hostility.
  • Spam, scams, trolling, or deliberate disruption.
  • Manipulation, bad-faith argument, or coercion.

Violations may result in immediate communication termination, access restrictions, platform reports, or further action where necessary.

Privacy & Boundaries

Privacy is mandatory. Do not attempt to collect personal information, publish private communications, or contact outside official channels unless explicitly invited.

Digital Boundaries

  • No attempts to access restricted site areas.
  • No bypassing security or manipulating protected content.
  • No invasive monitoring or tracking behavior.

Boundary violations may result in removal of access, reporting to hosting platforms or authorities, and legal action if required.

Changes, Terms, and Enforcement

This website, its content, and its policies may change at any time without direct notice. Changes are effective immediately upon publication.

By Using This Website, You Accept

  • Current and future policy updates.
  • That continued use equals acceptance of latest terms.
  • That earlier versions do not provide ongoing exceptions.

Enforcement

  • Restriction or removal of access.
  • Reporting to platforms or relevant services.
  • Legal action in serious breach cases.

If these terms are not acceptable to you, do not use or interact with this website.
