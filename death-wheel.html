5th Page Game

Death Wheel

Roulette survival mode. Place your bet, spin the wheel, and survive as long as your cash lasts.

How It Works

  • Start with $1200 and spin with fixed cost ($25).
  • Shop opens on Shop-slot landings and at predictable milestones (spin 6, 12, 18, ...).
  • Every 10 spins difficulty scales: +1 Death slot, +10% rewards, +10% penalties.
  • Run ends if money is $0, instant death triggers, lives reach 0, or you leave the page.
