Gambling King

Roguelite slot machine run. Start fresh every time, push your luck, and survive as long as possible before debt destroys your run.

Objective

Earn as much money as possible before you go completely bankrupt.

  • Every run starts with small money and no saved progress.
  • Spin to win or lose, then invest into upgrades.
  • Debt can keep your run alive, but too much debt ends everything.
